GERONTOLOGY
QUESTION: WHAT IS IT ABOUT

Gerontology is the study of the elderly, and of the aging process itself. The branch of science that deals with the problems of aged people, it covers the social, psychological and biological aspects of aging.

SOCIAL RESPONSIBLE SERVICES

The following are Free PROGRAMS/SERVICES offered to the Public
HIV/AIDS AWARENESS

HIV is a disease that does not discriminate. Anyone can contract it and hence why we all should be part of the program. The program is free to all and here you are equipped with knowledge to keep yourself safe from it as well how to cater for yourself or for a personal who might have contracted it.

HOME BASED CARE

We offer Home Care classes. We train students in professional support services to help a person needing special medical attention or is of old age to live safely in their homes.
